173 Human talk

Do you know that humans communicate when they talk?

Yes. When they call your name they want you not to pay attention.

I mean when they talk among themselves then it actually means something.

Well, maybe. Do you know what it means when they cry "Oh no! Not again!!"?

No.

It means "Bring more mice."
