Planet Earth: Cheetah

After the insane horror story we change the topic to a nature documentary.

Cheetah is the fastest cat on Earth.

A hungry female quietly approaches its prey.

A gazelle, the fastest antelope on the planet.

The cheetah waits for the perfect moment.

The hunt is on!

At such unimaginable speed, relativistic effects take place!

The time dilation and the space contraction allow...

...the gazelle to escape to safety.

Yet another unsuccessful hunt for the hungry cheetah...
