MORTKA AND MAŁYSZ

Remember the last winter?

Here comes the rain again...

I don't mind rain.

I prefer snow. Do you remember the last winter?

Aye! It was great!

The first steps in the snow...

Your red nose, when it's freezing!

Very funny!

I could hunt like...

...an arctic fox.

Do you remember a snowball supporting the rock?

Yes, yes... I do.

I fooled you!

Admit it!

No, you didn't. For I am a cat and I see things as they are not as they seem to be.
