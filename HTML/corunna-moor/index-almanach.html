List of species


2023

January
  • Blackbird - a few seen every day.
  • Roe deer - on and off the moor; sometimes seen on a few days in a row.
  • Magpie - a few seen every day; occasionally a large flock seen high on the trees.
  • Pigeon - many seen every day; almost exclusively on or around the spruces.
  • Robin - a few seen almost every day. In the gorse or in the woodland.
  • Rook - a few seen every day; mostly in the woodland.
  • Sparrow - several seen every day; in the hedge or in the cotoneaster.
  • Song Thrush - a few seen almost every day; in the gorse or on buddleia or on the cotoneaster.
  • Wren - a few seen every day; mostly in the gorse, but also in the woodland.
