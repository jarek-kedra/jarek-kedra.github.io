Jarek Kędra

Invariant geometry of groups


This is a book project on the geometry of groups equipped with bi-invariant metrics. We mostly consider groups normally generated by a finite set equipped with the corresponding maximal bi-invariant word metric.


Versions:
  • 2022-05-31
  • 2022-02-28


Apart from the survey of both known and new material, the book contains results obtained by me in collaboration with my excellent coauthors:

Misha Brandenbursky, Światosław Gal, Bastien Karlhofer, Assaf Libman, Michał Marcinkowski, Ben Martin, Egor Shelukhin, Alexander Trost.

I am grateful to them for the fantastic intellectual adventure.
